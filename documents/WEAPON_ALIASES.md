## üí° Tips

1. **Case insensitive**: `x HALLOW` or `x hallow` both work
2. **Spaces or dashes**: `x blazing staff` or `x blazing-staff` both work
3. **Full names always work**: If alias doesn't work, use full name
4. **Not listed?**: Use `/weapons` to see full weapon name
